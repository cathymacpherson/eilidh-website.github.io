- Cocktail Making -

After ceramics painting we’ll be heading to The Carmelite Hotel, located at Stirling St, Aberdeen AB11 6JU. Here we are going to do cocktail making!

The cocktail making class takes place in the Balmoral room at a private bar and lasts approximately 1.5 hours. Drinks wise, each person will receive an arrival Bellini, two cocktails, and a tequila rose shot.
